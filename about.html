About Crafted by Juanita

Crafted by Juanita is a small, community-focused craft studio creating custom gifts and collectibles. Every item is made with intention, blending classic handmade care with modern creative tools.

Juanita creates products using 3D printing and sublimation machines, allowing clients to personalize colors, themes, names, and sports-inspired details. The result is meaningful work that feels both practical and personal.

Her clients include women who enjoy crafts, thoughtful gifts, and sports collectibles that celebrate the teams and memories they care most about.

What Makes Her Work Special

Custom-first approach

Each order is designed around your ideas, colors, and occasion.

Modern craft process

3D printing and sublimation help deliver sharp details and durable finishes.

Warm customer experience

Friendly communication and craftsmanship built around real people and stories.
