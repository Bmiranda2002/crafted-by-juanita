Meet the maker

Juanita Moraila Stubbs

Juanita is the creative owner behind Crafted by Juanita, where handmade design meets practical gifting. She builds custom craft products for clients who love heartfelt details, sports-themed items, and personalized keepsakes.

Using 3D printing and sublimation equipment, she produces pieces that are colorful, durable, and tailored to each customer. Her goal is simple: make every client feel seen through thoughtful craftwork.

Through her Facebook community, Juanita shares new creations and takes custom requests for celebrations, collectors, and everyday moments.
